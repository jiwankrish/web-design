About Me

Hi, I'm Scott Thompson, known professionally as Carrot Top. I'm excited to share more about myself with you. I'm a stand-up comedian and actor, recognized for my unique style and prop comedy.

I've been entertaining audiences for years with my hilarious and innovative routines, often incorporating a wide range of props and visual gags. My comedy shows have brought laughter to countless people around the world.

While I'm known for my comedy, I also have a passion for philanthropy and have been involved in various charitable endeavors over the years.

Comedy and Career

I began my comedy career in the late 1980s and quickly gained recognition for my energetic performances and signature use of props. My comedy style is characterized by witty observations, clever wordplay, and a knack for turning everyday objects into comedic gold.

I've performed in top comedy clubs and theaters, including headlining at famous venues like the Comedy Cellar and the Laugh Factory.

Comedy and Career

Outside of comedy, I'm dedicated to giving back to the community. I've supported numerous charitable organizations and causes, using my platform to make a positive impact on the world. Some of my philanthropic efforts include:

  • Organizing charity comedy shows to raise funds for various causes.
  • Supporting children's hospitals and organizations focused on child welfare.
  • Advocating for environmental conservation and sustainability initiatives.
  • Mentoring aspiring comedians and artists.
